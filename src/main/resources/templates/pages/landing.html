About the Department

The Department of Land Resources, under the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India, administers programmes and policies relating to land resources. This Institutional Memory Portal is designed to preserve, organise, and provide secure access to the department's institutional knowledge and historical archives.

The portal provides integrated tools for document management, officer records, archival retrieval, and comprehensive analytics — all in one secure, government-grade solution.
